About Me

Darien Lizano is a student of Public Administration currently obtaining a Masters degree and a certificate in Data Analytics and Visualization at Seton Hall University. Following the completion of a course in statistical methods, he became passionate about using programming softwares for data analytics. He has, since then, pursued learning other languages such as Python and SQL, as well as visualization tools such as Tableau and Power BI.

To him, the most interesting aspect of the field of Data Analytics is its capacity to solve real world issues. Darien believes that data analysts should be able to think quickly and adapt to any environment to accomplish their tasks. He believes that data, adaptability, and collaboration are key to solving issues that businesses and organizations face on a daily basis.
